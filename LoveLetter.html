Love at first sight

Dear crush,
From the moment I met you, there was something about you that captured my heart.

Can you guess what is it...?? 😍
  1. Your kind smile
  2. Infectious laughter
  3. The way you light up a room with your presence
All make me feel a warmth and happiness that I cannot fully describe.
It is as if, in your company, everything else fades away and all that matters is the connection we share.

Am i crazy..?

I find myself constantly thinking about you, daydreaming about the moments we could share together.
You have become the inspiration behind my smiles and the reason behind the fluttering butterflies in my stomach.
Your presence in my life has brought such joy and happiness that I never knew was possible.

How do you feel about me..?

I want you to know that my feelings for you are genuine and sincere.
I am willing to take the risk and hope that you feel the same way.
If not, I will cherish the friendship we have and continue to admire you from afar.

💗 But if there is a possibility of something more, I would be honored to embark on this journey with you, to explore the depths of love and create beautiful memories together.💗

Wish to hear from you soon!

Final words, I have a gift for you.. Hope you like it..

Yours truly,
Muhammad Akram
